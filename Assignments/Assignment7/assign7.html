Assignment 7

This Tableau dashboard features interactive maps and a bar chart showing information gathered from the International Coffee Organization on
worldwide coffee production. A link to the data source is also embedded in the organization logo. The two maps show coffee exports and domestic
coffee consumption in coffee producing countries. The maps use the same measure per 60kg bags for easy comparison. They are able to be filtered
by year, region, month of crop year beginning, and/or type of coffee bean produced. The chart showing the coffee price estimation by various
groups and indicators also responds to changes in the year filter.
